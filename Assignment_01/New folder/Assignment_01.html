Calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) with this calculator. Your BMR is the amount of energy you expend each day when at rest. Gender, age, height and weight are important for this calculation. You have two calculators to choose from; metric and imperial. Note that both calculators use the Harris-Benedict equation to calculate figures and Once you know  your BMR, you can calculate your Daily Calorie Needs based on your activity level using the Harris Benedict Equation..
